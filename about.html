Thanks For Stopping By

After doing web development for 3 years, I've been drifting into a nice intersection of UX Design. It all started at my last web development job when I was often tasked with creating paper wireframes for clients and collaborating with fellow developers. My goal—whether it be with wireframes, mockups, or prototypes or development—has been to place myself in the user's shoes and try to create designs that will avoid any potential user pains and allow for the greatest user experience possible.
